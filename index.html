Okay, some more details...

  • Boot camp materials can be found at the GitHub repository.
  • Yes, you're invited no matter whether you're an incoming student, postdoc, faculty, or if you have been with KIPAC for a while! And yes, you can just go to the sessions that you're interested in.
  • No, no background in Unix or Python is required. You are very encouraged to attend the sessions on Wed and Thur if you are not familiar with Unix or Python.
  • We will try to make these sessions as hands-on as possible, so bring your laptop to the sessions.
  • For the Friday session, you can get to SLAC via free Marguerite shuttle. At the gate you might be asked where you are going. Just show them your Stanford ID and say you're going to the Kavli Building.
  • To access Internet at SLAC, connect to the "visitor" Wi-Fi and visit an HTTP webpage to sign in.
  • You can contact the organizer, Joe DeRose, at jderose [at] stanford [dot] edu.
